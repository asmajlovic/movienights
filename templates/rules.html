Movie night rules and regulations

  1. You don't talk about the movie nights.
  2. You do not talk about the movie nights.
  3. Every Thursday, pick a flick, turn up, and watch it - that's it.
  4. El Presidente:
    • Elected by members to preside over four shows.
      • Shows can be postponed only with a majority approval of associated/relevant voting members.
    • Determines whether excuses are valid or piss-poor (see Excuses and Multiplier sections).
    • Responsible for movie night correspondence and admin during their term.
    • El Presidente is expected to be present for a movie night. Failure to comply results in a multiplier adjustment, by the members present for that particular showing, to ANY permitted value deemed fit (see Multiplier section).
    • Eligibility (all criteria must be met):
      • A period of at least two terms must pass before the current El Presidente is eligible once again.
      • Members with a multipler score of one (or greater) are eligible for a presidential mandate.
  5. Excuses:
    • El Presidente to be informed and then determine if an excuse is valid (holiday, mum said 'no', pee-pee fell off).
    • Piss-poor excuses result in a single point drop of multiplier (see Multiplier section).
  6. Multiplier:
    • El Presidente tracking current multiplier status and handing off to newly elected one.
    • Permitted multiplier values are between 0 and 5
    • Previous attendance increases movie selection voting multiplier by 1, up to the maximum permitted value.
      • Intended to favour regularly attending members.
  7. Movie list:
    • Already fairly set as determined by the 'brain trust'.
    • Enough entries are already present in the list to last the next few years (or something to that effect), but if you really really think we're missing something please inform the other members and an ad-hoc discussion/vote can take place.
    • Selection (Doodle) process completed one day before showing.
  8. Movie selection:
    • May occur immediately after the preceding show.
    • A program picks 5 titles that have not been shown in the past.
    • A title that has been a previous candidate will not be eligible again until a period of two shows has passed.
    • Each member may vote for a single title only.
  9. Miscellaneous:
    • Bear in mind it could get complicated if the group gets too big.
    • Furthermore, refer to rule numbers one and two.
