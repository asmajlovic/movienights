Rules of the movie nights

  1. You don't talk about the movie nights.
  2. You do not talk about the movie nights.
  3. Every Thursday, pick a flick, turn up and watch it - that's it.
  4. President:
    1. Elected by members to preside for one month.
    2. Determines whether excuses are valid or piss-poor (see Excuses and Multiplier sections below).
    3. Responsible for movie night correspondence and admin during their term.
    4. A period of at least two months must pass before presiding member is eligible once again.
  5. Excuses:
    1. President informed and determines if an excuse is valid (holiday, pee-pee fell off).
    2. Piss-poor excuse results in a single point drop of multiplier (see Multiplier section).
  6. Multiplier:
    1. President tracking current multiplier status and handing off to newly elected one â€“ must be in the form of a PDF (!).
    2. Previous attendance increases movie selection voting multiplier by 1, up to a maximum of 5.
    3. Intended to favour regularly attending members.
  7. Movie list:
    1. Already fairly set as determined by the 'brain trust'.
    2. We have enough entries in the list for the next few years (or something like that), but if you really really think we're missing something please inform the others.
    3. Selection (Doodle) process completed one day before showing.
  8. Movie selection:
    1. Occurs immediately after preceding showing.
    2. A program picks 5 titles that haven't been showed in the past.
    3. Each attendee may vote for a single title.
  9. Bear in mind it could get complicated if the group gets too big. Furthermore, I refer you to rules number one and two.
